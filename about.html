Our Story

It's all started since 1920

Welcome to GoûtMélange, a restaurant with a story as rich and flavorful as the cuisine we serve. Our journey began over a century ago when our founder, a passionate chef with a love for fine dining, opened the doors to our first location. Since then, we have remained committed to using only the highest quality ingredients and techniques to create dishes that delight and satisfy our customers. Over the years, we've become a staple in the community, with generations of families enjoying our meals and creating memories within our walls. Today, our commitment to excellence remains as strong as ever, and we continue to innovate and evolve to meet the changing needs of our customers. Join us for a meal and experience the timeless elegance and exceptional flavors that have made GoûtMélange a destination for food lovers for over a century.

Our Goals

At GoûtMélange, our goal is simple: to provide an unparalleled dining experience that transports our guests to another time and place. We are committed to upholding the traditions and techniques of classical French cuisine, while infusing our dishes with modern and innovative flavors. Our team of expert chefs and sommeliers work tirelessly to source the finest ingredients and curate a wine list that perfectly complements each dish. We aim to create an atmosphere of elegance and sophistication, where guests can relax and savor every moment of their dining experience. Above all, our goal is to exceed the expectations of our guests and leave a lasting impression that will keep them coming back time and time again.
